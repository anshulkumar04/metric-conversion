Volume

Volume is a fundamental concept in physics and mathematics, referring to the amount of space that a substance or object occupies. It is a three-dimensional quantity, typically measured in cubic units such as cubic meters (m³) or cubic centimeters (cm³). In everyday terms, volume is used to describe the capacity of containers, the loudness of sound, or the amplitude of a signal. In the context of solid objects, volume can be calculated using various formulas based on the shape of the object, such as the formula V = l × w × h for rectangular prisms.
